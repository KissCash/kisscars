i20 Latest Update

Hyundai i20 Price: Hyundai retails the i20 between Rs 7.03 lakh and Rs 11.54 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi).
Hyundai i20 Variants: It can be had in four trims: RXL, RXL (O), RXT, and Climber.
Hyundai i20 Engine and Transmission: The i20 gets the same petrol and diesel engines as the Venue. The 1.2-litre naturally aspirated engine (83PS/114Nm) is paired to a 5-speed manual and an optional CVT, while the 1-litre turbo-petrol (120PS/172Nm) gets a 6-speed iMT (clutchless manual) and a 7-speed DCT. The 1.5-litre diesel unit (100PS/240Nm) is available with a 6-speed manual only.

Hyundai i20 Features:The carmaker has equipped it with a slew of features like LED headlights, a 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, an air purifier, BlueLink connected car tech, and a sunroof.
Hyundai i20 Safety: Safety features onboard include six airbags, rear parking sensors, and electronic stability control.
Hyundai i20 Rivals: Hyundai’s premium hatchback takes on the Honda Jazz, Tata Altroz, Maruti Baleno, and the Toyota Glanza.

Hyundai i20 Price

The price of Hyundai i20 starts at Rs. 7.03 Lakh and goes upto Rs. 11.54 Lakh. Hyundai i20 is offered in 21 variants - the base model of i20 is Magna and the top variant Hyundai i20 Asta Opt Turbo DCT DT which comes at a price tag of Rs. 11.54 Lakh.
