Civic Latest Update

Latest Update You can avail benefits of up to Rs 27,000 on the Civic this June.
Honda Civic Price: The compact sedan is sold between Rs 11.29 lakh and Rs 15.24 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi).
Honda Civic Variants There are three trims on offer: V, VX, and ZX. The Civic Hybrid is based on the top-spec ZX trim.

Honda civic Features:The carmaker has equipped it with a slew of features like LED headlights, a 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, an air purifier, BlueLink connected car tech, and a sunroof.
Honda Civic Engine and Transmission: Honda Civic Engine and Transmission: The Honda Civic gets a 1.5-litre petrol (121PS/145Nm) and diesel engines (100PS/200Nm). The former is paired to a 6-speed MT and an optional 7-step CVT, while the diesel unit is available with a 6-speed MT only. Honda has launched the Civic Hybrid in India which gets a dual-motor setup and offers an average mileage of 26.5kmpl.
Here are the claimed mileage figures:

  • Petrol MT: 17.8kmpl
  • Petrol MT: 17.8kmpl
  • Diesel MT: 24.1kmpl
Honda Civic Features: It gets an 8-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Android Auto, Apple CarPlay and Weblink support, 16-inch dual-tone alloy wheels, ambient lighting and connected car tech. Honda Civic Safety: Safety of the passengers is taken care of by six airbags, tyre pressure monitoring, ABS with EBD, and ISOFIX child seat anchors. Honda Civic Rivals: The Civic takes on the Hyundai Verna, Maruti Suzuki Ciaz, Skoda Slavia, VW Vento and the Volkswagen Virtus.
