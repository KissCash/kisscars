KWID Latest Update

Latest Update: Prices for the Renault Kwid have been hiked by up to Rs 13,000.
Renault Kwid Price: The Kwid’s new prices range from Rs 4.62 lakh to Rs 5.96 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi).
Renault Kwid Variants: It can be had in four trims: RXL, RXL (O), RXT, and Climber.
Renault Kwid Engine and Transmission: Renault has equipped the hatchback with a 0.8-litre (54PS/72Nm) and a 1-litre (68PS/91Nm) unit, both paired to a 5-speed manual as standard. A 5-speed AMT is available as an option with the 1-litre petrol unit.

Renault Kwid Features: The Climber variant of the Kwid packs 14-inch black wheels, electric ORVMs (outside rearview mirrors), and a day/night IRVM. The French carmaker also offers an 8-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, keyless entry, manual AC, and a reversing camera.
Renault Kwid Safety: Renault offers the Kwid with safety features such as dual front airbags, ABS with EBD, and rear parking sensors. It also gets a pretensioner and load limiter for the driver’s seatbelt in the Climber variant.
Renault Kwid Rivals: The Kwid is a rival to the Maruti Suzuki Alto and Maruti Suzuki S-Presso. Furthermore, its top-spec variant competes with the Tata Punch.

Renault KWID Price

The price of Renault KWID starts at Rs. 4.62 Lakh and goes upto Rs. 5.96 Lakh. Renault KWID is offered in 8 variants - the base model of KWID is RXL and the top variant Renault KWID CLIMBER AMT which comes at a price tag of Rs. 5.96 Lakh.
